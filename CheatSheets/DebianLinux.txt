-- Clicking the eject symbol in the Files GUI interface does more than
   unmount USB drives; it also renders their contents inaccessible.  If
   you want to get direct access to the bits on USB drives, you should
   use 'umount' (if it's been auto-mounted).  
   There's apparently an 'eject' command to use for this portion of
   the process (untried).
